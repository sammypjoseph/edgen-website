How the tax credit works

1
You give to EdGen

You contribute any amount up to your credit cap ($1,700 single / $3,400 MFJ — subject to final Treasury/IRS guidance) to EdGen's scholarship fund.

2
Your credit is applied

When you file your federal taxes, you claim a 100% federal tax credit equal to your contribution — reducing your tax bill dollar for dollar.

3
Scholarships are awarded

100% of your contribution (less nothing, since EdGen charges 0% admin) goes to eligible scholarship recipients at qualified schools.
